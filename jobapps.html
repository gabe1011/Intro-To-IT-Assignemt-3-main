Kaleid AI – AI Software Engineer

At Kaleid AI, we are helping create the future we want. With a small team of six we are excited to expand and take on big tech where we aim to disrupt the industry with innovative and modern technologies. To do this, we will need the best of the best, the hardest working, and the people who are excited to make real change in the world. We believe that Kaleid AI is your pathway to success in the technology space and we look forward to having you on board.

The opportunity

This full-time role is open to someone who wants to help build up and coming new software with a vibrant team. We are looking to find an enthusiastic, experienced software developer who will be willing to take on challenging software engineering tasks. We want you to help build out the future of memory recollection and storage using software solutions. You will be working with other skilled software developers who are like minded and skilled in different areas. You will have the opportunity to gain experience from others in a tightly knit office space where you will be given the chance to flourish. As we are still a small start-up, you will be able to bring your ideas to the table, to a team with open ears, to a group who is willing to try new things.

Qualities required for a successful application

  • Diligent

  • Team-work outlook and focus

  • Flexible and agile when it comes to adapting to new situations

  • Good attitude

  • Open to learning

  • Abundant in ideas

  • Excited for the future of technology

    Skill requirements

  • At least 2 years' experience in software development

  • Experience using .NET / C#, MySQL, and SQL Azure

  • Deep understanding of databases, data management and data analysis

  • Ability to articulate complex problems and share to teams

  • Preferably a degree in computer science, IT, or another related field

    A successful candidate

    A successful candidate will have all the above qualities and skills and will demonstrate this in a professional programming test. You will also be required to answer questions about yourself in an in-person interview with the Chief Development Officer (CDO). Thank you for applying to Kaleid AI, we look forward to having you working here!
